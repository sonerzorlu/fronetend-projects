Being a great developer means that you can communicate effectively with users and other developers. In fact, a large part of being an excellent programmer is sharing your ideas effectively and efficiently. If you can present your ideas clearly and concisely, others will want to listen to what you have to say. But communication isn't just about talking. It's also about listening to what others have to say. Studies show that good listeners were more successful in their careers than good talkers. So you should never underestimate the power of a good listener, especially when working with clients or other developers on your team. Learning how to listen can be invaluable, especially if you're planning on working with other people.
